Welcome to Helena's

Serving traditional, local Hawaiian food since 1946 this family owned business has impacted locals and

tourists alike around the world. Come dine with us and experience the spirit of aloha with our ohana!

In 1946, Helen Chock opened the doors to Helena's Hawaiian Food, a humble eatery on North King St. in Honolulu, Hawaii.
